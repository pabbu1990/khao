Getting started
Your kitchen, live in
about 10 minutes
Five simple steps — no tech skills needed.
1
Create your account
Go to thekhao.com and tap Get started. Sign up with email or Google and name your kitchen — you’ll get your own link, thekhao.com/your-name, right away.
2
Build your menu
On your dashboard, tap Create a menu (like “Weekend Specials”), then Add dishes — each with a price and an optional photo. Add as many menus as you run.
3
Set payments & pickup
In Settings, choose how customers pay — Cash and/or Interac e-transfer (add the email/phone they send to). Add your pickup address and your logo while you’re there.
4
Share your link
On your dashboard, open Your ordering link → Copy it or share by text, WhatsApp or Instagram. You can also show or print the QR code.
5
Take orders
Every order lands on your live dashboard the moment it’s placed. Accept it, mark it ready, and mark it paid once the money’s in. That’s it.
Good to know
Mark a dish sold out in one tap — it updates on every customer’s screen. Order counts reset daily. Download your orders as a report anytime. You keep 100% — Khao never takes a commission.
